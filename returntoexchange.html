Return & Exchange

General Guidelines

  • The product must be unused, unworn, and unwashed. Fashion products can be tried on for fit and will still be considered unworn.
  • The return period for defective or exchanged items is 15 Working Days from the date of receiving the parcel.
  • Please include a copy of the invoice and clearly mention the reason for return.
  • Customers must return merchandise via traceable delivery (courier or registered post) at their own expense.
  • Delivery charges are non-refundable.
  • Refunds are processed within 10 working days after we receive the returned product.
  • Clearly label the package as "Return Item" to avoid processing delays.
Note: No refund or exchange on Baby Care and Feeding items due to hygiene issues.
