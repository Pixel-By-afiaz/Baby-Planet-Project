Shipping Policy

We are committed to delivering your order accurately, in good condition, and always in time. After placing an order, you will receive a confirmation call/email from our Representative. Once confirmed, it will immediately be dispatched.

10:00 am – 8:00 pm

Free Shipping

On all orders exceeding

Coverage

Shipping available all over Pakistan.

Premium Shipping: Baby Planet also has Premium Shipping available for your convenience.

COD Method: To curtail fraudulent, all orders are shipped on Cash on Delivery (COD) payment method.

Absence Policy: If the customer is absent, our representative will call. The parcel can be delivered at another time or dropped at neighbors only upon receiving the payment.

We amend our policies time to time, please stay updated with us.
