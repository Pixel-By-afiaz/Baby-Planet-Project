Order Placement

You can submit an order for products by completing the details required on the order summary page and clicking the "Place Order" button. We also welcome orders placed via phone call or direct message.

⚠️

Important Note

The display of any product on our website is an invitation to treat, not a direct offer to sell. Your order represents the offer. We reserve the right to decline sales for products quoted at a price lower than intended due to technical errors.

✉️

Acceptance of Order

A binding agreement is formed once we notify you of our acceptance via email. If we cannot accept your order or if the price changes before acceptance, we will contact you immediately via email to advise you of the situation.

🚚

Delivery Policy

We strive to deliver all products within 3 working days of order acceptance. In the event of unforeseen accidents or mishaps, delivery times may be extended; we will keep you informed via phone or email.

💵

Payment

For standard delivery, payments are collected via Cash on Delivery (COD). We maintain a transparent pricing policy—there are no hidden charges.

🛡️

Warranties & Returns

All our products are covered under our warranty section. If a product is not up to the mark, we encourage you to return it. We provide a full refund and will bear the shipping costs for such returns.
